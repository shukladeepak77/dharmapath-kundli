What is a Shubh Muhurat?

A Muhurat is an auspicious time window selected by Vedic astrology principles for starting important activities — marriage (Vivah), house warming (Griha Pravesh), business inauguration, travel, naming ceremonies and more. A good Muhurat combines a favorable Tithi, Nakshatra, Vara (weekday) and Choghadiya period.
