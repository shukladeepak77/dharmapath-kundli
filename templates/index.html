What is a Jyotish Kundli?

A Kundli (also called Janam Patri or Vedic Horoscope) maps the positions of the nine planets (Navagraha) at the exact moment of your birth. It is the foundation of Jyotisha — the ancient Indian science of light — and reveals insights about your life path, dharma, career, relationships and spiritual journey.
