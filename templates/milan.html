What is Ashtakoota Guna Milan?

Ashtakoota Milan is the classical Vedic method of assessing marriage compatibility. It evaluates eight qualities (Koota) — Varna, Vashya, Tara, Yoni, Graha Maitri, Gana, Bhakoot and Nadi — carrying a combined maximum of 36 Gunas (points). A score of 18 or above is generally considered suitable for marriage.
