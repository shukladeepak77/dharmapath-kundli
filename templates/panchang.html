What is a Panchang?

Panchang (Sanskrit: पञ्चाङ्ग) is the Vedic almanac that records five key time elements for each day: Tithi (lunar day), Nakshatra (lunar mansion), Yoga (lunisolar combination), Karana (half-day period) and Vara (weekday lord). It is the foundation for selecting auspicious timings (Muhurat) in Vedic tradition.
